About Us

Goa Tourism Development Corporation Limited (GTDC) was set up on 30th March, 1982 to look after the commercial activities of the Government in the services industry of tourism in the state. The liabilities of Government in the form of accommodation (hotels), vehicles, tours, boats and other properties were transferred to Goa Tourism Development Corporation Ltd to run and to manage the same with a view to promoting, developing in the state and to carry out business and to manage the welfare of the employees transferred along with the assets of the Government Goa Tourism Development Corporation. Ltd has come a long way and completed 25 years of successful operation in tourism sector and is one of the successful Corporation in the service industry in the State of Goa.
The company is geverned by the Board of Directors appointed by the Government. The Board Of Directors consists of 12 members. The shareholders consist of 7 members, all of whom are Government nomiees. All the ahares are held by the Governor of Goa, except 7 shares which are held by its nominees. The Managing Directors is the Executive Head of Goa Tourism Development Corporation Ltd.
